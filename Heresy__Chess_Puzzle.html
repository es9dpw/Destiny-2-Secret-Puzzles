Destiny 2 Secret Puzzles

Chess Puzzle

Information

During the completion fo this puzzle, you will receive 3 guaranteed shiny spet weapons, so it is recommended to max out both of your paths at the shaping slab do these weapons drop with triple perks in each column, rather than just double.

You are required to have collected all 60 chess pieces in order to complete every puzzle, if you have not collected them all, use Esoterickk's video here.

To place a chess piece stand on whatever piece you want to place, and then shoot the place where you want to place it.
