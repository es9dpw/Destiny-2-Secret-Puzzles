Sundered Doctrine Quest

Complete the Sundered Doctrine dungeon. This only has to be a final boss checkpoint if you wish, so go grab a checkpoint and finish the dungeon.

Step 3

After Collecting these 3 worms you must restart the dunegon and enter the 3 symbols shown in the image below: Witness, Give, Dark

After this, run forward to the area before the 1st encounter where you will see a plate. You must charge the playe by killing enemies that will drop dread knowledge, pick up this knowledge and stand on the plate to deposit it. Complete the Sundered Doctrine dungeon. This again only has to be a final boss checkpoint if you wish, so go grab a checkpoint and finish the dungeon.

Step 6

Step 5 was auto-completed as this quest used to be timegated, so we move straight on to step 6.

Enter the Hidden's camp at the start of the dungeon and face the wheel, from there turn around and look at the tables and there should be a piece of paper to pick up.

There are now 3 more worms you must pickup throughout the dungeon. All 3 are again shown in vidoes below, with the 1st being before the 1st encounter, the 2nd being after the 1st encounter but before the 2nd encounter, and the 3rd being after the 2nd encounter but before the final encounter.

After Collecting these 3 worms you must restart the dunegon and enter the 3 symbols shown in the image below: Hive, Grief, Worm

After this, compelete the 1st and 2nd encounters and go forward to the area where you picked up the 2nd worm after the 2nd encounter. You must go ahead and go under where you would normally go to leave the area, and then you will run back and find a knight which will drop a worm when killed, which must then be brought back to where you first went to and deposited. Then move back and the worm will explode, opening a hole with a pedestal inside that you cn interact with to complete this step of the quest. The full puzzle is shown in the video below:

Complete the Sundered Doctrine dungeon. This again only has to be a final boss checkpoint if you wish, so go grab a checkpoint and finish the dungeon.

Step 9

Step 85 was auto-completed as this quest used to be timegated, so we move straight on to step 9.

Enter the Hidden's camp at the start of the dungeon and face the wheel, from there to the right and look at the crates and there should be a piece of paper to pick up.

There are now 3 more worms you must pickup throughout the dungeon. All 3 are again shown in vidoes below, with the 1st being before the 1st encounter, the 2nd being after the 1st encounter but before the 2nd encounter, and the 3rd being after the 2nd encounter but before the final encounter.

After Collecting these 3 worms you must restart the dunegon and enter the 3 symbols shown in the image below: Guardian, Knowledge, Fleet

After this, compelete the 1st encounter and go forward past the maze. You must bounce the light from the lenses around the building in the middle back to the front of it, by rotating so lenses and killing lens bearers to drop a lens and place it. Once the light is bounced all the way around a door will open with a pedestal that you can interact with to complete this step. The full puzzle is shown in the video below.

Step 10

Restart the dunegon and enter the 3 symbols shown in the image below: Worm, Give, Guardian

After this complete the dungeon in the current run, you cannot go to orbit. After beating the dunegon a door will open at the back of the final boss room. In this room will be a chest which will complete the step when opened.

Step 11

Talk to Ikora in the tower to complete the quest.
